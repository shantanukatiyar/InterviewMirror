TRANSPARENT EVALUATION

How Your Practice Score Is Calculated

Your score is generated from your actual spoken-answer transcript and timing data. It is designed to support practice, not to judge intelligence, personality, or employability.

25%

Answer Completeness

Checks whether your response has enough spoken content for a meaningful answer.

25%

Relevance Signals

Checks for useful interview-related keywords connected to the selected question type.

20%

Answer Structure

Checks response organization or STAR-method signals for behavioural questions.

15%

Speaking Pace

Estimates words per minute from your transcript and answer duration.

15%

Filler-Word Control

Counts common filler words such as “um,” “uh,” and “like.”

Important: InterviewMirror uses browser speech recognition and rule-based analysis. It does not verify factual correctness or replace feedback from a human interviewer.

COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

How is my score calculated?

The score uses your transcript, answer length, relevant keywords, answer structure, estimated speaking pace, and filler-word usage.

Does InterviewMirror record or store my voice?

No. The project uses browser speech recognition for the current session. Your latest report is stored locally in your browser.

Will it work on every browser?

Speech recognition works best in recent Chromium-based browsers such as Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge. Microphone permission is required.

Does this replace a real interview?

No. It is a practice tool that helps you build answer structure and speaking habits before real interviews.
